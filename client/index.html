Features

Everything you need in a modern innovation hub and more.

Empowering Innovation

100% dedicated to fostering innovation and technological advancement. The Ministry of Innovation and Technology in Ethiopia is at the forefront of the nation's digital transformation. From policy development to supporting startups, the ministry ensures that Ethiopia stays ahead in the global tech landscape.

Watch Ethiopia Innovate

The MoIT actively promotes innovation across all sectors. By supporting research and development, the ministry empowers young entrepreneurs and innovators to turn their ideas into reality. This commitment is helping Ethiopia grow into a hub of innovation and creativity.

Tech for All Inclusive Digital Transformation

The ministry’s initiatives focus on ensuring that all citizens, regardless of location or background, have access to the benefits of digital transformation. Through partnerships with local and international stakeholders, MoIT provides resources, training, and opportunities to bridge the digital divide.

Operations

Everything as simple as possible, but no simpler.

Simplifying Innovation

MoIT creates clear and effective policies that foster innovation. These policies aim to remove obstacles for innovators and businesses, making it easier for them to thrive in Ethiopia's tech landscape. By focusing on straightforward and actionable policies, MoIT ensures that the process of innovation is accessible and streamlined.

Empowering Citizens

MoIT works to digitize government services, making them more accessible and user-friendly for the general population. Whether it’s providing online platforms for public services or ensuring internet access in remote areas, MoIT simplifies how citizens interact with technology and government services.

Supporting Growth

MoIT provides essential resources, mentorship, and funding to startups. The ministry simplifies the process of starting and scaling a business by offering easy access to support systems. This ensures that entrepreneurs can focus on innovation without getting bogged down by complex administrative procedures.
